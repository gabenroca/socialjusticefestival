DAY 1
Guest Speakers
With expert speakers, participants can expect dynamic discussions on recognizing and addressing cyberbullying, understanding the importance of digital etiquette, and learning practical strategies for creating a positive online environment for everyone to enjoy.
Food For All
Grab a bite! Combine the joy of good food and advocating against cyberbullying. Proceeds will go directly to non-profit organizations fighting this issue as well as schools across the nation. Food trucks will serve diverse and delicous foods to satisfy every palette.

DAY 2
Scavenger Hunt
Students will navigate through the city streets, unlocking the next level of the scavenger hunt with every successful discovery. Students use their phone camera to capture landmarks, solve riddles, and complete creative challenges, all while racing against the clock. It is an immersive experience that encourages teamwork, creativity, and a bit of friendly competition.
Game Night
Come join us at Smile Online's Game Night, where the thrill of victory and the joy of socializing combine to create an atmosphere of pure entertainment and competition. Students play both board games and video games, making friends and pushing positivity. Let the games begin!

DAY 3
Concert
Smile Online's concert is about creating a communal experience where students become part of something extraordinary. Feel the crowd and let the music take over you. Music that spreads a good messgae from artsits that stand against online hate and byberbullying.
Performances by Selena Gomez, Bruno Mars, Coldplay, Lady Gaga, and more!
